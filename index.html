Decision Guide

Which Brand Should You Choose?

Each brand excels in a specific context. Here’s the quick-match guide.

💼

Choose DF If…

You need the best value with the widest size range in Australia — from compact 28×10mm to large-format 90×130mm — and the most ink colour variety. DF is StampStore’s own brand, available in 11 colours including Fluro Red and Sky Blue.

Ideal for: Offices, schools, admin teams, address stamps, budget-conscious buyers
🏭

Choose Trodat If…

You stamp hundreds of documents daily and need a stamp that doesn’t miss a beat. Trodat’s Austrian engineering — shock absorbers, locking cap, text-view window — makes it the benchmark for warehouses and high-volume offices. Snap-in ink pad system makes refilling fast.

Ideal for: Warehouses, logistics, legal & professional offices, high-frequency stamping
🌿

Choose Colop If…

Precision alignment, modern aesthetics, and environmental responsibility matter to you. Colop’s transparent positioning windows make accurate placement easy. Many models use recycled materials. Quality matches Trodat at a comparable price.

Ideal for: Design professionals, eco-conscious businesses, precise alignment requirements
🎨

Choose Trodat Multi-Colour If…

Your brand or project demands colour. Trodat’s Multi-Colour stamps produce up to 15 distinct colours in a single press (conditions apply), with smudge-free results. Available in compact Printy Series and larger Professional Series.

Ideal for: Brand stamps, event management, creative studios, colour-coded documentation
